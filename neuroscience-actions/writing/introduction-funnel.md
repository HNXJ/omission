# Neuroscience Writing: Introduction & Context Funnel

Setting the biological and clinical stage for circuit modeling studies.

## 1. The Disease Landscape
- **Broad Context**: Define Schizophrenia as a cognitive and sensory processing disorder involving positive, negative, and cognitive symptoms.
- **Circuit Deficits**: Introduce the hypothesis that cellular-level GABAergic changes underlie functional deficits observed in MEG/EEG.

## 2. Cellular Candidates (PV & SST)
- **Interneuron Diversity**: Briefly define Parvalbumin (PV) and Somatostatin (SST/CB) interneurons.
- **Laminar Specificity**: State that deficits are not uniform but layer-specific (Mulvey et al., 2025).

## 3. The Modeling Gap
- **Problem Statement**: Standard clinical imaging lacks cellular resolution; traditional models lack biological detail.
- **Our Solution**: Introduce Jaxley-based biophysical modeling and AGSDR optimization as a bridge between cell-density meta-analyses and functional oscillations.
- **Specific Hypothesis**: Explicitly state H1 ($\downarrow$ PV $\to$ Weak Gamma) and H2 ($\uparrow$ CB $\to$ Enhanced Beta).
