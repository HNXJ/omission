## Phase 6 Roadmap: Precision Scoring (The GPA Protocol)

### 1. The Significance-Tier Standard
We define the **Significance Tier ($S_k$)** to provide logarithmic resolution of effect sizes:

| Tier | P-Value Range | Designation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Null** | $p \ge 0.5$ | No Effect |
| **Insignificant** | $0.5 > p \ge 0.05$ | Trend / Weak |
| **Sig-1** | $0.05 > p \ge 0.01$ | Significant (*) |
| **Sig-2** | $10^{-2} > p \ge 10^{-3}$ | High Significant (**) |
| **Sig-k** | $10^{-k} > p \ge 10^{-(k+1)}$ | Sovereign Significant (k*) |

### 2. The 50-Point Checklist (Draft)
