# Omission Project Skills: Comprehensive Inventory

This document inventories the specialized skills and knowledge domains required for the Omission project. These skills represent the agentic capabilities used to navigate, analyze, and build the repository.

## 🔬 Scientific Analysis & Domain Knowledge (Science)

### Overview
The scientific core of the project focuses on demonstrating that omission responses are active prediction errors rather than passive sensory decays.

### Skill Inventory:
- **science-neuro-omission-active-inference**: Precision scaling and predictive quenching.
- **science-neuro-omission-cortical-hierarchy**: Information flow from V1 to PFC.
- **science-neuro-omission-ghost-signals**: Quantification of sensory-independent generative signals.
- **science-neuro-omission-identity-coding**: Content-specific information in omission windows.
- **science-neuro-omission-prediction-errors**: Formal mapping of mismatch signals.
- **science-neuro-omission-spectral-fingerprints**: Frequency-domain motifs of predictive routing.
- **science-neuro-omission-surprise-latencies**: Temporal dynamics of unexpectedness.
- **science-neuro-omission-variability-quenching**: Fano factor and variance reduction analysis.

## 📊 Neural Analysis & Mathematics (Analysis)

### Overview
Advanced mathematical and statistical methods for analyzing high-dimensional electrophysiology data.

### Skill Inventory:
- **analysis-neuro-omission-effective-connectivity**: Directed influence (Granger, PDC).
- **analysis-neuro-omission-functional-connectivity**: Statistical dependencies (Coherence, PLV).
- **analysis-neuro-omission-oculomotor-suite**: Saccade and micro-saccade suppression.
- **analysis-neuro-omission-pac-analysis**: Cross-frequency coupling (Theta-Gamma).
- **analysis-neuro-omission-population-manifolds**: State-space trajectories (PCA, UMAP, jPCA).
- **analysis-neuro-omission-pupil-decoding**: Physiological markers of arousal and surprise.
- **analysis-neuro-omission-unit-classification**: Polarity-based clustering (S+/-, O+/-).
- **analysis-spectrolaminar**: CSD-based layer mapping and crossover detection.
- **math-neuro-omission-connectivity-metrics**: Formal connectivity implementation.
- **math-neuro-omission-stochastic-metrics**: Variance normalization and stability.
- **nwb-analysis**: General NWB data exploitation patterns.
- **spike-population**: Population-level spiking dynamics and aggregation.
- **eye-behavior**: Integration of oculomotor data with neural signals.
