## 3. Statistical Test Methodology
### a. Stability Constraint
- ISI violations < 0.5%.
- Continuous monitoring: Average firing rate must exceed 1.0 Hz in every single individual trial of the RRRR condition.

### b. Polarity Significance (S+ / O+)
- **Test**: Two-sided Related Samples T-Test (ttest_rel).
- **Alpha Correction**: Bonferroni family-wise correction ($p < 8.278 \times 10^{-6}$).
