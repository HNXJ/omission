# FIG_20: Global Flow of Omission Information

## 🎯 Intent
To synthesize the directed information flow across the entire 11-area hierarchy, visualizing the multi-area coordination during the omission response.

## 🔬 Methodology
- **Source**: `src/f020_effective_connectivity/analysis.py`
- **Method**: Directed Graph Analysis based on Granger Causality.
- **Metric**: Net Information Flow ($FF - FB$).
- **Visualization**: Circle plot or Hierarchical Flow-Map.

## 📊 Observations
- Hierarchical routing: Net feedforward flow propagates from V1 through the visual hierarchy (V4, TEO) to PFC.
- Synthesis: The omission event triggers a global "surge" in feedforward error signaling across the cortical mantle.

## 📝 Caption & Labels
**Figure 20. Global Topography of Effective Connectivity during Omission.**
(A) Multi-area graph showing directed information flow between 11 hierarchical levels.
(B) Transition matrix for connectivity states (Baseline vs. Omission).
