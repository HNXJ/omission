# FIG_04: Representative Coding Units & Manifolds

## 🎯 Intent
To show the single-unit structure of the omission response and how it contributes to the stable population manifold.

## 🔬 Methodology
- **Source**: `src/f004_coding/analysis.py`
- **Visualization**: Raster plots and spike density functions for "Ultimate Stable Units" (GPA 100).
- **Metric**: Comparison of Standard (AAAB) vs. Omission (AXAB) coding fidelity.

## 📊 Observations
- "Ghost Neurons": Identification of units that fire specifically for omissions but show minimal stimulus-driven activity.
- Robust coding: High-GPA units maintain precise temporal lock to the expected stimulus time even in its absence.
