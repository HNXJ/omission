# FIG_06: Hierarchical Scaling of Band Power

## 🎯 Intent
To quantify the hierarchical gradients of oscillatory power modulation during the omission event.

## 🔬 Methodology
- **Source**: `src/f006_band_power/analysis.py`
- **Bands**: Theta (4-8Hz), Alpha (8-13Hz), Beta (15-25Hz), Gamma (40-80Hz).
- **Metric**: Z-scored power modulation relative to pre-stimulus baseline.
- **Population**: All 11 areas categorized by hierarchical index (1-11).

## 📊 Observations
- Systematic increase in omission-locked gamma power as a function of hierarchical depth.
- PFC and FEF exhibit the most robust beta-suppression signatures.
