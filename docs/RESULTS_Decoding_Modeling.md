## Results: State-Space Decoding and RNN Derailment

Using an SVM classifier, we decoded omission versus standard stimulus trials with >90% accuracy in high-order frontal areas, confirming that the omission creates a distinct, reproducible cortical state. 

### RNN Manifold Derailment
Computational modeling via a Reservoir Computing RNN corroborated these findings. The model replicated the empirical population trajectory, where omission-linked sequences caused a abrupt "derailment" of the state-space manifold. Mapping slow-timescale reservoir dynamics to higher-order areas and fast-timescale units to sensory areas showed that this derailment is a fundamental consequence of hierarchical predictive routing.
